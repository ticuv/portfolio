Țicu Vlad (ticuv) - Professional Visual Designer from Iași, Romania

Țicu Vlad, known professionally as ticuv, is a skilled visual designer specializing in poster design, 3D art, generative design, and brand identity work. Based in Iași, Romania, Vlad offers freelance design services for startups, small businesses, and creative agencies worldwide.

Design Services Offered by ticuv:

  • Poster Design: Swiss style posters, brutalist design, minimalist posters, and experimental typography
  • 3D Art & Visualization: Abstract 3D renders, product visualization, digital sculptures, and 3D graphics
  • Logo Design & Branding: Custom logo design, brand identity systems, visual communication, and brand strategy
  • Generative Art: Algorithmic design, creative coding, parametric art, computational design, and data visualization
  • Additional Services: Motion graphics, packaging design, editorial design, social media graphics, print design

Why work with Țicu Vlad (ticuv)?

With a foundation in photography since 2022, ticuv brings a unique perspective to digital design. Combining discipline with creative energy, Vlad creates impactful visuals that help brands stand out. Whether you need a modern logo, striking poster designs, or complete brand identity, ticuv delivers professional, agency-quality work at freelance rates.
